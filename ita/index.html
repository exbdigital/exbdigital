Notes on the consulate's rules

  • Submit all documents in original and copy — originals are returned, except bank statements.
  • Do not purchase flight tickets before the visa is granted — a reservation or hold is enough.
  • A forged or fake document will not be returned, and can lead to prosecution in both Italy and Nigeria.
  • The consulate may request additional documents beyond this list at its discretion.
