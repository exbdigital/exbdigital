Apply now, not later. A Standard Visitor visa can only be applied for up to 3 months before travel — for a 20 Oct 2026 trip, that window opened around 20 July 2026. Standard processing is about 3 weeks (15 working days) from your biometric appointment, so there's no benefit in waiting.
Digital by default now. Since 25 Feb 2026, UK visit visas for Nigerian applicants are issued as an eVisa (a digital immigration record), not a sticker in the passport. You'll need to create a UKVI account and link it to your passport before travel to view and prove your status — passports themselves are now returned faster since nothing needs to be physically stamped. Applications still go through gov.uk first, with biometrics booked separately at visa.vfsglobal.com/nga/en/gbr — VFS Global runs centres in Ikeja and Victoria Island (Lagos) and Abuja, plus newer Premium Application Centres in Enugu, Port Harcourt and Kano. Optional VFS add-ons (Form Filling assistance, Premium Lounge, SMS Alerts) cost extra and don't affect the outcome or timeline.

Only the 3 Nigerian-passport travellers pay the visa fee — your daughter doesn't need a Standard Visitor visa at all if she travels on her Canadian passport, since Canadian citizens are visa-exempt for UK visits under 6 months. She only needs the £20 ETA (valid 2 years, unlimited entries). This is materially cheaper and simpler than applying for her on the Nigerian passport instead.

With your ~3-month lead time, Priority/Super Priority services add cost with no real benefit — standard processing will comfortably clear before 20 October. Government fees are non-refundable regardless of outcome. Always confirm the exact figure on visa-fees.homeoffice.gov.uk before paying, as this is the only authoritative source.

Application timeline

Now — ~20 Jul 2026

Open online applications

Create a GOV.UK account and complete the Standard Visitor visa form separately for you, your spouse, and your son. Each gets a GWF reference number. Start your daughter's UK ETA application at gov.uk/eta/apply — it's a separate, much shorter process.

Same session

Pay the fees online

Pay each visa fee at the point of application. The ETA fee is paid within its own app/online flow.

Within a few days

Book VFS Global biometric appointments

Book at visa.vfsglobal.com/nga/en/gbr — centres include Ikeja and Victoria Island (Lagos) and Abuja, plus newer Premium Application Centres in Enugu, Port Harcourt and Kano. All 3 visa applicants attend to give fingerprints and a photo, and submit supporting documents. You don't need multiple copies of shared documents when applying as a family. Services like Form Filling assistance, a Premium Lounge slot, or SMS status alerts are optional add-ons — they cost extra and have no bearing on the decision.

~3 weeks after biometrics

Decision (standard service)

Standard processing is approximately 15 working days. The ETA decision is typically much faster — often within minutes to 3 working days.

Before 20 Oct 2026

Create your UKVI account & confirm your eVisa

Passports are returned to you shortly after a decision — there's no vignette sticker to wait for. Each of the 3 visa holders must create a UKVI account and link it to the exact passport used, then confirm their eVisa status shows correctly before travelling. Separately, confirm your daughter's ETA is approved and linked to the same Canadian passport she'll travel on.
